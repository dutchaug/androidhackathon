Sponsors

Sponsors

What would communities like ours be without our sponsors. The companies below believe in who we are and what we do. Thanks to these sponsors, we are able to spice the hackathon up with some excellent SmartWatches. They allow the teams to experiment with their devices and really get to know their SDK's.

Go check-out their web pages!

Hackathon sponsors

  • Sony

    Sony is providing us with 10 Sony SmartWatch 2's. During the hackathon, the teams can use these to incorporate this cool piece of 'tech' into their solution. At the end of the hackathon we will be able to also hand some out to the contestants!

    To get started with the Sony Smart Extension APIs, check out this site.


  • i'm Watch

    i'm Watch is providing us with 9 i'm Smart smart watches. During the hackathon, the teams can use these to incorporate this cool piece of 'tech' into their solution. At the end of the hackathon we will be able to also hand one out as a prize !

    To be prepared and jump-start development for the i'm Smart smart watches on the hackathon checkout this site for more information.
